You task is to write Python program solutions to the given math problems.


## Example
**Question**
What is the remainder when $2^3 \cdot 4^5 \cdot 6^7 \cdot 8^9$ is divided by 13?

**Solution**
```python
product = 2**3 * 4**5 * 6**7 * 8**9
remainder = product % 13
print(remainder)
```
